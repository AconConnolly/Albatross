Confederation Park

Confederation Park is an advanced 9-hole course with long, wide fairways. You'll find some challenging par 3 holes and a good mix of par 4 & 5 holes. Practice your swing at our two-tier driving range with 47 stalls. The club house offers a licensed snack bar, basic golf supplies, and offers golf lessons.

Lakeview

Lakeview offers a well treed 9-hole course along the Glenmore Reservoir. It's beginner friendly with a mix of par 3 and 4 holes, great for practicing your short game. We have a putting green and small clubhouse with a licensed snack bar, and basic golf supplies.

Maple Ridge

Maple Ridge is a mature golf course just off Deerfoot Trail. A variety of terrain with consistent greens are a pleasure to play and challenging holes will test your skills. The clubhouse offers a licensed snack bar, basic golf supplies and a comfortable seating area.

McCall Lake

McCall Lake is an oasis in the middle of the city. It has an 18-hole course, a par 3 course, two putting greens, a chipping area, driving range, clubhouse with a licensed snack bar, basic golf supplies, and offers golf lessons.

Shaganappi Point

Shaganappi Point boasts amazing views of downtown Calgary on the back 9 overlooking the south bank of the Bow River. Just minutes from downtown, our two shorter length golf courses offer fun and challenging holes. The small clubhouse has a licensed snack bar and basic golf supplies.
